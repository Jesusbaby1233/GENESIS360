Genesis360

Genesis360 is a digital retail company that
provides consumer credit in the form of food
loans. We enables farm-to-retail distribution for
local/small-scale farmers. Aggregating supplies
of farm produce and making it accessible to the
final consumer. Our BNPL offering enables
individuals to purchase food items immediately
without payment or initial deposit and allow
them to pay back before a 30-day period
without interest, or in installments over 3 months
with interest.
